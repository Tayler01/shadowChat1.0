# Shadow Runner Concept Art

Generated review anchors for Shadow Runner enemy, villain, and gameplay asset planning.

## 2026-06-09 Sheets

- `enemy-character-sheet-2026-06-09.png`: lighthearted enemy roster with idle, movement, attack, hit, and defeated pose anchors.
- `villain-pose-sheet-2026-06-09.png`: larger mini-boss and boss pose sheet with suggested frame counts and bottom-center anchors.
- `game-asset-sheet-2026-06-09.png`: terrain, platforms, hazards, collectibles, props, mobile HUD icons, and small VFX anchors.

These are concept/reference sheets, not final transparent sprite strips. Next production step is to choose the approved characters/assets, generate fixed-frame strips, and normalize each strip to a shared bottom-center anchor.
